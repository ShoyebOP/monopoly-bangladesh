## Guidelines for Property Mapping

1. **Maintain Price Tiers**: Cheap US properties → smaller/less developed Bangladeshi areas
2. **Geographic Logic**: Consider grouping by division or geographic proximity
3. **Cultural Significance**: Important landmarks/cities for expensive properties
4. **Recognition**: Use well-known place names for better player engagement
5. **Pronunciation**: Ensure Bangla names are easy to read and pronounce
