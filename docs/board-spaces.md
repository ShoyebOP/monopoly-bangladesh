## Area Rationale

### Low Income Areas (Brown, Light Blue)
- **Kuril Basti, Karail Basti**: Large slum areas, lowest income
- **Mirpur, Mohammadpur, Uttara**: Working class residential areas

### Middle Class Areas (Pink, Orange, Red)
- **Tejgaon, Khilgaon, Magbazar**: Old residential/commercial mix
- **Malibagh, Rampura, Basabo**: Developing middle class areas
- **Dhanmondi, Green Road, Elephant Road**: Established upper middle class

### Wealthy Areas (Yellow, Green, Dark Blue)
- **Kalabagan, New Market, Paltan**: Central wealthy areas
- **Shahbag, Karwan Bazar, Gulshan-1**: Elite commercial/residential
- **Banani, Bashundhara**: Most expensive, ultra-modern areas
