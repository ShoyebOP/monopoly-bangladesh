## Notes

- All cards use Taka (৳) symbol for money values
- Numbers in card text use Arabic numerals (1, 2, 3) for universal readability
- Bangla text is written in formal but accessible language
- Cultural adaptations maintain the spirit of original cards while being relatable to Bangladeshi players
