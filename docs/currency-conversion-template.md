## Conversion Methodology Notes

1. **Direct Multiplication**: Multiply USD amount by exchange rate
2. **Rounding**: Round to nearest 10 or 100 for easier gameplay
3. **Consistency**: Use same rate throughout all conversions
4. **Playability**: Consider simplifying numbers for mental math

---

## Conversion Formula

```
BDT Amount = USD Amount × Exchange Rate

Example: $200 × 100 = ৳20,000
```
