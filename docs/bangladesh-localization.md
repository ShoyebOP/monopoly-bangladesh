# Bangladesh Localization

## Overview
Complete localization of Monopoly to Dhaka, Bangladesh context. All properties mapped to Dhaka areas based on economic status.

## Localization Principles

1. **Economic Matching**: Properties mapped to areas with similar economic status
2. **Geographic Logic**: All areas are within Dhaka city for coherence
3. **Recognition**: Well-known area names for player engagement
4. **Cultural Relevance**: References adapted for Bangladeshi context
5. **Pronunciation**: Names are commonly known and easy to pronounce

## Area Descriptions

### Slum Areas (Brown)
- **Kuril Basti**: Large informal settlement near Kuril
- **Karail Basti**: One of Asia's largest slums, near Gulshan

### Working Class Areas (Light Blue)
- **Mirpur**: Large residential area, working class
- **Mohammadpur**: Dense residential, affordable housing
- **Uttara**: Northern suburb, developing area

### Lower Middle Class (Pink)
- **Tejgaon**: Industrial-residential mix
- **Khilgaon**: Old residential area
- **Magbazar**: Commercial-residential hub

### Middle Class (Orange)
- **Malibagh**: Traditional middle-class area
- **Rampura**: Developing residential area
- **Basabo**: Quiet residential neighborhood

### Upper Middle Class (Red)
- **Dhanmondi**: Established affluent area
- **Green Road**: Commercial-residential, upscale
- **Elephant Road**: Major commercial area

### Wealthy (Yellow)
- **Kalabagan**: Central affluent area
- **New Market**: Commercial hub, wealthy
- **Paltan**: Central business district

### Very Wealthy (Green)
- **Shahbag**: Cultural-commercial elite area
- **Karwan Bazar**: Major business district
- **Gulshan-1**: Diplomatic zone, ultra-wealthy

### Ultra-Wealthy (Dark Blue)
- **Banani**: Modern commercial-residential, expensive
- **Bashundhara**: Newest, most expensive residential area

## Notes

This localization maintains the game's economic structure while making it relatable to Bangladeshi players, particularly those familiar with Dhaka's geography and socioeconomic landscape.
