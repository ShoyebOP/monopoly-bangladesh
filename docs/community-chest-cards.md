## Cultural Adaptations Made

| US Reference | Bangladeshi Equivalent | Rationale |
|--------------|----------------------|-----------|
| Old Kent Road | Kuril Basti | Cheapest property on the board (matches original intent) |
| Stock | Share/Stock | Same concept, widely understood |
| Holiday fund | Holiday fund | Urban Bangladeshis save for holidays |
| Income tax refund | Income tax refund | Common concept in urban Bangladesh |
| Life insurance | Life insurance | Widely understood in urban areas |
| Hospital fees | Hospital fees | Private hospital fees are common |
| School fees | School fees | Education expenses are universal |
| Consultancy fee | Consultancy fee | Growing professional culture |
| Street repairs | Street repairs | Common in Dhaka city context |
| Beauty contest | Cultural program | More general, fits Bangladeshi context |
| Inheritance | Inheritance | Universal concept |

## Notes

- All money values use Taka (৳) symbol in Bangla text
- Movement instructions are in simple Bangla
- Cultural references adapted for Bangladeshi context (cultural program instead of beauty contest)
- Location names match the board spaces (Kuril Basti as the cheapest property)
- Birthday tradition is common in Bangladesh (collecting from friends/family)
